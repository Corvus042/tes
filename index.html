What is this Website?

Ever notice a crow's inclination to accumulate hordes of trinkets? Stygian little dragons perched upon heaps of shiny do-dads and thingy me-bobs. You see dear reader, I'm very akin. Anything and everything with a remote glimmer that stirs at my interest I must have, and this goes far beyond the material. I mainly collect skills, interests, and ideas. Of course, my bookcase can hold my books and my trinkets I collect, but there is no room for the products of the mind there. My art, music, ideas, research and more all are adrift across different islands. This website is my curation of passions. It is a horde which is ever growing, reaching higher and higher. This is largely for me but I welcome all to come and shift through the lovely junk I've produced and collected.

Click on contact for email and social and click on skills to see what this old dog can do!!
